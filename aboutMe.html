About Me

Computers have been a part of my life since I was a little kid. I remember my grandmother bringing home a room full of computers that she had purchased from an old computer lab from a local school. This was back when computers ran on MS-DOS and you had to boot them with a floppy disk.

I remember the many endless hours sitting on her living room floor with the manuals tinkering away when I was about 10.

It also sparked the salesperson in me when I started using that knowledge to fix other family member's computers to make spending money in middle school. I became certified at John A Logan College and my entry point into the tech world was repairing and troubleshooting the computers for local small businesses. I’ve been in the space for 6+ years, working at Hype Media for 3 of them later as a DevOps Engineer for a non-profit.
